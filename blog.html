Home Maintenance: Your Key to Savings and Safety

Home maintenance is essential for protecting your largest investment. Regular upkeep prevents small issues from becoming expensive problems—a $200 roof leak can become a $5,000 disaster if ignored. This comprehensive guide covers monthly interior and exterior checks, seasonal tasks for spring through winter, and critical annual services. Learn when to call professionals versus DIY projects, and discover how preventative maintenance saves money on utilities and increases home resale value. By creating a personalized maintenance schedule and staying consistent, you'll avoid emergency repairs, maintain energy efficiency, and preserve your home's structural integrity for years to come.
